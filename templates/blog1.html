Brief overview of Ezio Auditore

    Ezio Auditore da Firenze is the most known character of the Assasins Creed Series.He is a fictional character from the italian renaissance.

Ezio's Birth

Ezio was Born on 1479 at firenze(now florence).His father was Giovanni Auditore, an accountant and a secret assasin.He was the second child of giovanni.He was from a wealthy family.Until he was 17, he didn't had any tension and lived a happy life with his family , until his father and big brother had been hanged due to Rodrigo Borgia.

Ezio's appereance in Assasin's Creed

Ezio has been a main character in 3 games, Assasin's Creed 2(2009), Assasin's Creed Brotherhood(2010) and Assasins creed Reveleations(2011)
